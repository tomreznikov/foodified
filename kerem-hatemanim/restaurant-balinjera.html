Balinjera

Kosher Ethiopian cuisine located in the heart of Kerem Ha’Teimanim that will make you feel right at home. Each dish is served with traditional, gluten-free bread called Injera, that is made with Ethiopian Teff flour. “Balinjera” translates to fellowship, as Ethiopian food aims to join together culture, flavors, and colors.
