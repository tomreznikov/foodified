La Repubblica

La Repubblica, an urban-italian restaurant, serves an authentic Italian menu. The food is local, seasonal and fresh, with a strong connection to the rich Italian culinary heritage. Along side the displayed cheeses, cold cuts and famous anti-pasti, homemade pastas are served, fresh pizza is baked, meat and fish get the most of expertise care.
