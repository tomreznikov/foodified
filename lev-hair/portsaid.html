Port Said

Port Said is a restaurant, a bar and a record library. The menu was created by Eyal Shani, one of israel's most known chefs, with the aim to create a casual affordable version of his culinary vision. We do not reserve tables, so feel free to drop by for a drink, a bite and a dosage of super vibes.
