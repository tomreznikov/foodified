Okinawa

For 13 years, the Okinawa restaurant on 46 Shabazi Street has been an exotic and inviting culinary gem in the heart of the enchanting and picturesque Neve Tzedek neighborhood. The restaurant is designed as a neighborhood bar sushi that serves its guests a delicious taste of Far Eastern delicacies, seasoned with a wealth of experience and love.
