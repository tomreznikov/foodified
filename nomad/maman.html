Maman

With a “you’re-in-Paris” feel, this cozy bakery/cafe offers tasty French and American bites for breakfast and lunch, plus sweet stuff à la its famous chocolate chunk cookie; the cute, shabby-chic space has rustic wood tables and a few outdoor seats. Try the warm egg bowls.
