Cucina Hess 4

A while ago, chef Tamar Cohen-Tzedek’s popular Italian restaurant Cucina Tamar became simply Cucina. So she found a way to ease back into the routine: Hess 4 is now open for dinner two evenings a week – Wednesdays and Thursdays. “After all,” Cohen-Tzedek says, “I’m a mother as well.” The venue is very small, with six tables inside, seating just 14, and another three on the sidewalk, a few steps away from the corner of Allenby Street. The atmosphere is cozy but not exactly intimate, as the tables are a bit close together. And because the food is so good, the place is almost always full.
