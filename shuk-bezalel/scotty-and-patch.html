Scotty and Patch

Once the promenade around the Beit Rubin Museum is over, visit Scotty and Patch. At this restaurant, you will be offered French and Mediterranean cuisines. Serving mouthwatering salads is the feature of Scotty and Patch. Here you can drink good wine. The warm atmosphere of this place makes guests feel relaxed and have a good time. The gracious staff reflects the style and character of this spot. Fabulous service is something clients like here. Based on the reviewers' opinions, prices are attractive.
